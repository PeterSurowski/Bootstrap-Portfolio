about me

Peter Surowski is a freelance web developer based in Southern California. He's also a journalist and a professor of Web Design.

Peter's specialty is growth. He loves to work with an inbound/content marketing team to design websites that bring customers in the door.

He writes-mostly about the coffee and tech industries-for a variety of publications.

He also teaches art students how to build websites at the University of Redlands.
